About: Hotel Mist Tree Mountain

Located in the capital, Gangtok, of the mountain state of Sikkim is Hotel Mist Tree Mountain. The hotel, established in 1991 has been engaged in the hospitality business for over 20 years. There are a total of 19 double occupancy rooms of varying categories. The tasteful design of the hotel, plus its location, and magnificent view has ensured repeat guests and steady referrals from old guests as well as travel agents.......

Centrally located in Gangtok, the hotel offers urban convenience – a few minutes walk to shops, restaurants, night clubs, live music, local sights and the famed MG Marg. But once ensconced in the beautiful, serene view facing rooms, one forgets all about the urban jungle outside.
