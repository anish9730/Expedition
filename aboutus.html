About My Compny

Expedition Travels and Leisure is a fast growing global DMC (Destination Management Company), serving the burgeoning B2B segment of the travel and tourism industry for Thailand.

Driven by customer-centric operating model, we put to work our industry experience, extensive destination knowledge -how, expertise to deliver a quality experience. At the same time, we aspire to outperform our previous successes via continuous improvement and consistently endeavor to provide value to our clients. With an emphasis on reliability, safety, and scalability, lasting bonds are fostered with our esteemed customers, industry partners, and associates.

Expedition Travels and Leisure service portfolio encompasses Leisure FIT, Leisure Groups and Special Interest Groups. Right from offering an exciting bouquet of dynamic and ready packages, contracted & OTA hotels, apartment, villas, attractions, restaurant and in-house fleets and more to come, we offer the complete spectrum of services across the DMC value chain for Thailand, Srilanka & Singapore.

We empower customers by furnishing accurate destination and contextual information while extending reliable travel management support to our various travel groups. Simultaneously, we are steadily striving to achieve greater competencies in this domain.
